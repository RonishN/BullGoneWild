Take Control of the Chaos

Bull Gone Wild is a fast-paced arcade action game where you play as a raging bull tearing through chaotic arenas.

You build momentum, chain attacks, and slam into enemies as everything around you explodes into chaos. It’s all about feeling powerful, out of control in a good way, and turning every fight into something loud, messy, and fun.

  • Play as a Bull: Take control of the chaos instead of running from it.
  • Build Momentum: Combo kills fill your momentum bar.
  • High Scores: Compete for the highest combo chain and best times.
